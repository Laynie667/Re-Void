The blue pulsing of the letters |mAuria|n is not a battery — the material is photoluminescent, charged by the room's light. In the dark, it pulses on its own for hours.

roomzone detail stage/pole = The steel pole is chrome-finished and cold to the touch, the surface smooth to the point where it has almost no friction. The plugs are cleaned and oiled — the silicone surface slightly glossy. The vibrators have their control units beside them in a specific orientation, power switches accessible. The beads in the dish are loose but coiled, the last bead of the string tied off in a knot that is meant to hold.

roomzone detail accessories/paddles = The paddles on the pegboard are arranged by weight — lightest at left, a flat-leather piece; a rubber paddle with holes at center; a heavier wooden paddle at right with a slight flex to the strike face. The cane is thin, spiral-cut, the kind that marks rather than bruises. The crop is the longest item on the wall, its loop end worn at the edge. All items are within arm's reach of each other but arranged so each one can be taken without disturbing the others.

roomzone handle collar_wall/collar = You lift the titanium collar from its chain. It is heavier than jewelry, lighter than structural metal — the weight that means something was built to a purpose. The blue letters pulse against your palm. You bring it to your throat and hold it there, not closing the clasp, and in the mirror across the room you can see yourself doing this. The collar's interior neoprene is at body temperature. You close it with the two-stage mechanism — ring, then bar — and for a moment there is the very clear knowledge that you cannot open it from this side. You stand there with the knowledge for a moment, then release the bar and ring in sequence and set it back on the chain.

roomzone handle horses/pommel = You approach the pommel horse and run a hand along the center leather. The surface is smooth in the way of something kept conditioned. You find the twin dildo mount beneath your palm — the forward angle of it orienting your hand correctly for the geometry the device is designed for. You look at the shackles. At arm-spread, at this height, wrists locked in those shackles would leave you suspended with your feet just clearing the floor. The calculation is in the chain length. Someone was specific.

roomzone handle stage/pole = You step down to the stage and put your hand on the pole. The chrome is mirror-smooth, the temperature neutral. You turn with it, and in the mirror you see the room behind you: the horses, the swing, the collar wall, the couches facing the stage.
