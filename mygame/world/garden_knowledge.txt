roomzone handle shelves/grimoires = You pull the horizontal volume from the top of the grimoire section and it comes away with the slight resistance of something that has been placed with intention and does not quite want to be disturbed. The cover is warm — not from the room's ambient heat, which is general and even, but with a localized warmth that fades as you hold it, as though whatever was keeping it warm has decided to wait. Inside: handwriting in at least three different inks on paper of at least four different ages, the annotations so dense on some pages that the original text is barely visible. You put it back. The resistance when you replace it is different from the resistance when you took it: easier, as though it has decided to accept you.

roomzone handle shelves/mlp = The volumes are what they are — explicit, occasionally tender, frequently inventive in their extrapolation of source material into territory the source material did not contemplate — and they have clearly been read with genuine investment. The sticky-note flags mark pages that meant something specific to whoever placed them. You read a flagged page. You set it back with the same care you'd give anything belonging to someone who loves it.

roomzone handle desk/notes = You pick up the topmost page — one of the wet-spotted ones — and hold it carefully. The ink where the moisture fell has bloomed outward, the letters still readable but softer now, the precise small handwriting given a slight imprecision it did not have before. The content is still legible: a passage of composition, something between a journal entry and a letter, the recipient not named. The wet spots have not altered what it says, only how it looks saying it. You set it back where it was, damp edge facing inward, the same way it was.

roomzone handle beanbag/wear = The beanbag receives you the way it has received its primary occupant: the left lean is already built in, the posture waiting for you without requiring instruction. The fill is warm. The fabric has the particular softness of something washed often, and the scent at the back-left is specific — not perfume, the accumulated self of someone who has spent many hours here. You settle into the impression and it is entirely comfortable in a way that feels slightly like trespass. The coloring books are within reach. The books above are within reach. The desk is visible. This is what it looks like from here.

roomzone handle floor/stuffed_animals = The rabbit comes off the floor with the slight resistance of something that has been in its place a long time and is comfortable there. One eye is a replacement — a different button, close in size and color, sewn on with the same care as the re-stitched ear. The original eye is gone and whoever replaced it matched as closely as they could, and the slight difference between the two eyes is not a flaw but a repair, which is a different thing. You set it back in its exact position. It is important that it goes back in its exact position.
