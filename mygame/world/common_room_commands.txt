Following the tracks from left to right, they grow closer together — a quickening pace — and at the rightmost end of the beam they simply stop, mid-stride, as though the wolf stepped off the edge of the wood and into the room. On the mantle's surface: a |wmatched pair of pillar candles|n, mostly burned; a |xsmall carved figure|n of a wolf sitting upright; and a |xphoto in a simple frame|n that faces the fire rather than the room.

roomzone detail hearth/loveseat = The loveseat is sized for two people who don't mind being close, or one person using the full width with intent. roomzone detail hearth/recliner = The recliner is the most honest piece of furniture in the room. roomzone detail dining/table = The cup rings in the finish cluster at two ends of the table — the chairs pulled out most often, the seats worn smoother than the others. The groove along the northern edge is deliberate: shallow, smooth, worn by the same motion repeated often. A thumb, or the edge of a palm. Someone who sits on that side and leans in when they talk.

roomzone detail dining/flowers = The wild flowers in the vase are fresh within the last few days — a mix of whatever was growing near the treeline, stems uneven, nothing arranged with particular intention. There is no card. Someone picked them and put them in water and that was the whole of it.

roomzone detail island/note = The handwritten note under the salt shaker is folded once. The handwriting visible on the outside edge — neat, deliberate — belongs to someone who writes the way they do everything else. The note is not addressed to you.

roomzone detail island/fridge_contents = The refrigerator is well-stocked in the way of a house where people actually cook: |xcondiments|n ranked by frequency of use, |xleftovers|n in labeled containers, a full |wvegetable drawer|n, wine on the door shelf. One shelf on the right side has been given over entirely to small sealed jars — pale, cream-colored contents — labeled in the same neat hand as the whiteboard list. The labels do not explain. The shelf is consistently full.

roomzone detail kitchen/whiteboard = The whiteboard on the refrigerator door is in two handwritings. The first — neat, organized — lists what appears to be a meal plan, partially completed, one day crossed off with a clean line. The second handwriting is looser, slanted, and has added several items to the list that are not groceries, interspersed with the actual groceries without any apparent awareness of incongruity. roomzone detail kitchen/herbs = The drying herbs near the kitchen window are tied in small bunches — lavender, rosemary, something with a sharper green smell that is harder to name. They have been there long enough to be properly dry. Someone put them up deliberately and has left them deliberately. The window they hang near faces north, which gets no direct sun, which is correct for drying.

roomzone detail floor/scorch = There is a second scorch mark on the hearthside floor, half-hidden by the rug's edge. Smaller than the one on the dining table. Older.
