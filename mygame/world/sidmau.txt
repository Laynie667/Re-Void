-- STEP 8: Handle Interactions --

roomzone handle exterior/surface = You press your palm flat against the SIDMAU's shell and the surface returns nothing — no warmth, no cold, just your own temperature given back to you at the same value. The faint topology you thought you saw resolves under touch into something that may be imagined: a depth to the surface that is not physically possible, the way a very still body of water looks deeper than it is. You pull your hand back. There is no residue on your palm, no mark on the surface. The point of contact has already been reclaimed.

roomzone handle door/authorization = You bring your fingertip to the authorization region — the warmth gives it away before anything else — and hold it there. Nothing happens. Or: everything happens at once, the door recessing into the shell without sound, the warm mineral air of the interior meeting your face with the slow specificity of something that has been kept and is now released. The pressure equalization makes a sound that the room absorbs immediately. You stand at the threshold of the interior and the darkness inside is not absent of light but is something that light does not apply to in the ordinary way.

roomzone handle interior/temperature = The walls are body temperature the moment you brush them — there is no adjustment period, no sensation of warming, just the immediate, slightly uncanny experience of a surface that is already you. The ceiling above in its egg-curve is imperceptibly warmer. The fluid at your feet is cooler by a margin that registers as intention. You become aware of the gradient as a system: lower, cooler; upper, warmer; your body held between the two at exactly the temperature it came in with. The door closes — you did not close it — and the room's hum disappears, replaced by the unit's own silence, which is not the same thing as quiet.

roomzone handle fluid/surface = The fluid surface yields under your first contact without the cold shock that water carries — it meets you at your own temperature, already calibrated, the way it knows what you are and makes itself amenable. |mYou lower yourself further and the fluid does not displace so much as accommodate: your weight finding purchase not on anything solid but on the pressure of the medium itself, even on all sides, a support that comes from everywhere and therefore feels like nowhere.|n Below the surface the mineral quality is more pronounced — it enters through the sinuses when you float, specific in its chemistry, neither pleasant nor unpleasant in the way that very clean things are neither. You cannot feel the bottom because there is no bottom to feel.

roomzone handle panels/inputs = The port accepts the lead without resistance — slides in to the fitting depth and holds, the seal immediate. Inside the unit, the lead's far end has emerged from the interior wall within reach, and the electrode pad is already in position for placement. |mYou understand, now, what the unit's intelligence is: not the fluid, not the walls, not the door — but this. The knowing of where the body is, and where it needs to be, and the arrangements it has already made for that.|n The ADAPT monitor outside ticks up one increment.
