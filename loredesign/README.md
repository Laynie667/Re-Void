# loredesign/

**Worldbuilding & lore blueprints for what proves out.** Characters, factions, places,
history, cosmology, the relationships and tone — written up cleanly once they've earned their
place in the live game, so the fresh build inherits a settled, consistent world.

One file per subject (e.g. `bethany.md`, `seraphine-vesper-calix.md`, `the-facility.md`,
`kakia-and-the-cabin.md`, `timeline.md`). Capture: who/what it is, the canon details, voice/
tone, relationships, how it shows up in play, and what's locked vs still-flexible.

> Contents here are **gitignored** (except this README) — keep working lore on your local
> machine; promote finished canon into the tracked repo when building the fresh copy.

Keep it consistent with the established voice (CLAUDE.md §5) and the in-fiction/OOC split
(the §0 floor is always Laynie's, never gated).
