What is Re:Void

Re:Void is a text-based roleplaying world built out of nessecity, inspiration, and loneliness. You are not a hero. You are not some empty thing to be cast aside.

You are a memory given flesh — a fragment of something that refused to stop existing.

In this patch of void, the rules of before mean nothing.

Reality is sculpted in prose and ink. Factions form not only for war, but for family and companionship.

This is not some game you win. This is a world you haunt.
